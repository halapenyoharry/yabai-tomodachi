## Maker Comment

👋 Hey Product Hunt!

After years of using Yabai, I kept hitting the same frustrations - windows getting stuck, forgetting commands, and wishing I could just describe what I wanted.

yabai-tomodachi (友達 = friend) is my solution. It's a menu bar companion that makes Yabai friendly and adds AI superpowers through Claude integration.

My favorite feature: You can now say "set up my coding workspace" instead of memorizing 10 different keyboard shortcuts!

It's 100% free and open source. I'd love your feedback on what window management problems you'd want AI to solve next!

## FAQ Section

**Q: Do I need Yabai installed first?**
A: Nope! yabai-tomodachi includes an installer that helps you set up Yabai if you don't have it.

**Q: How does the AI integration work?**
A: It uses Claude's MCP (Model Context Protocol) to let you control windows with natural language. Just install Claude Desktop and add our MCP server.

**Q: Is it really free?**
A: Yes! It's open source (MIT license) and will always be free. Built for the community!

**Q: What makes this different from other Yabai GUIs?**
A: AI integration, focus on the most common pain points (like quick restart), and a vision for universal tool protocols beyond just window management.

**Q: Can I contribute?**
A: Absolutely! Check out our GitHub - we welcome PRs, feature requests, and bug reports.
